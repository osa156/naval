Our Story

About Grace Community

Building a community of faith, hope, and love since 1999.

Our Mission

Transforming Lives Through Christ

We exist to help people find their way back to God, connect in authentic community, and make a difference in the world.

Our Vision

To be a thriving community where every person discovers their purpose, develops their potential, and deploys their gifts to serve others.

Our Mission

Leading people to become fully devoted followers of Christ through worship, community, service, and multiplication.

Our Values

Authenticity, Excellence, Generosity, and Compassion guide everything we do as a church family.

What We Believe

Our Statement of Faith

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, authoritative Word of God and is trustworthy for faith and practice.

Salvation

We believe salvation is a gift of God's grace through faith in Jesus Christ, not earned by human effort.

The Church

We believe the Church is the body of Christ, called to worship, fellowship, discipleship, ministry, and evangelism.
